### Transport

WARN: This will remove your current .config

```bash:~/
sudo rm -rf ~/.config
cp -r ~/downloads/dotfiles/.config ~/.config
```

### Install Prerequisites

```bash:~/downloads/dotfiles/scripts
cd dotfiles/scripts
sh install.sh # bash install.sh || ./install.sh
```
